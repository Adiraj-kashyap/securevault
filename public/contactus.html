Frequently Asked Questions

What is SecureVault?

SecureVault is a comprehensive security platform that helps you manage passwords, secure messages, and protect your digital identity. Our state-of-the-art encryption ensures your sensitive information remains private and secure.

How secure is my data with SecureVault?

We use military-grade AES-256 encryption to protect your data. Your information is encrypted locally on your device before being stored, meaning even we cannot access your sensitive information. We also implement two-factor authentication, secure password hashing, and regular security audits.

What if I forget my master password?

If you've set up account recovery options in your settings, you can use those to regain access. Otherwise, for security reasons, we cannot recover your master password. This is why we strongly recommend setting up recovery options and keeping a secure backup of your master password.

Can I access SecureVault on multiple devices?

Yes, SecureVault is designed to work seamlessly across all your devices. Your encrypted data syncs securely between devices, allowing you to access your passwords and secure messages from your computer, phone, or tablet.

Is there a limit to how many passwords I can store?

No, there is no limit to the number of passwords you can store in SecureVault. Our service is designed to accommodate all your password management needs, whether you have a few dozen or hundreds of passwords.

How do I set up two-factor authentication?

You can set up two-factor authentication in your account settings. We support various 2FA methods including authenticator apps, SMS verification, and security keys. This adds an extra layer of security to your account, requiring both your password and a second factor to log in.
